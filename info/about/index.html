About BSR Studios

Empowering Windows users with lightweight, highly-optimized freeware applications.

Our Story

BSR Studios was founded with a simple frustration: modern PC screen recorders had become too resource-heavy. We noticed that most popular recording software encodes video in real-time. While convenient, it silently drains system memory, spikes CPU usage, and causes severe frame drops during gaming or resource-heavy tasks.

We believed that users shouldn't have to choose between recording their screen smoothly and keeping a fast computer, especially those running on budget setups or older hardware. That’s when the idea for Any Screen Recorder was born.

The Any Screen Recorder Vision

Any Screen Recorder was built from the ground up to solve the performance issue. By ditching real-time heavy video encoding and developing a method to capture raw screen data directly into a temporary .screc file, we successfully created a screen recorder that captures your desktop flawlessly without adding extra load on your processor.

Once you are done recording, the software compiles this raw data into a standard MP4 file. From version 1.0.0 to our latest updates, our mission remains the same: deliver smooth recording with a minimal system footprint.

Our Core Values

Performance First

Every line of code is optimized. By separating the capture process from the encoding process, we ensure your system performance is maintained at all times.

100% Freeware

We believe basic PC utility software should be accessible to everyone. Our core applications are free to use, and we never place watermarks on your recorded videos.

Privacy Focused

Your screen recordings and personal data belong to you. Our app runs entirely locally on your machine. We do not collect, track, or upload your videos anywhere.

User-Centric Design

We listen to our community. Features like delayed video processing, WASAPI audio capture, and cursor highlighting were implemented directly from user feedback to solve real daily problems.
