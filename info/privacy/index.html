Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Here is a clear explanation of how BSR Studios handles your data.

Last Updated: April 2026

TL;DR: The Any Screen Recorder desktop application does not track you, does not collect personal data, and does not upload your recorded videos anywhere. It runs entirely locally on your machine.

1. Introduction

Welcome to BSR Studios ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy applies to our website (anyscreenrecorder.github.io) and our desktop software application, Any Screen Recorder.

2. The Any Screen Recorder App

We believe that PC utility tools should not compromise your privacy or system performance. Therefore, the Any Screen Recorder desktop application operates with strict privacy standards:

  • Local Processing & Storage: All screen capturing, temporary .screc file saving, and MP4 video compilation happen 100% locally on your computer's hardware. We never upload, host, or have access to your recorded videos.
  • No Telemetry: We do not track your desktop usage, what games or apps you record, or how long you use the software.
  • No Background Spying: The app does not collect personal identifiers, hardware IDs, or browsing history.

3. Information Collected on Our Website

When you visit our website, we may collect basic, non-personally identifiable information strictly to understand how our site is performing:

  • Download Tracking: We use an anonymous counter API (hosted on Cloudflare Workers) to track the total number of Any Screen Recorder downloads. This does not store your IP address or personal data.
  • Contact Forms: If you use our Contact Us page, your Name, Email, and Message are processed securely via Web3Forms and sent directly to our support email. We only use this information to reply to your inquiry.

4. Third-Party Services

We use a few trusted third-party services to host our website and manage communications. These services have their own privacy policies:

  • GitHub Pages: Used for hosting our website securely.
  • Web3Forms: Used strictly for routing contact form submissions to our email inbox.
  • Cloudflare Workers: Used for securely managing our anonymous download counter API.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect any information you share with us (such as via email). However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy notice from time to time as we add new features to Any Screen Recorder or as laws change. The updated version will be indicated by an updated "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions or comments about this policy, or if you need to report a privacy concern, please contact us via our Contact Page or directly at aryanmewadastudios@gmail.com.
